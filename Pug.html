Pug

Pictures Of The Pug

What You Need To Know

  • Sleeping hours per day: 14 hours
  • Avrage Size: 10 inches to 1 foot, 2 inches tall at the shoulder
  • Average Weight: 14 to 18 pounds
  • Personality : Pugs are clowns at heart but walk with dignity. They are dogs that love to be in the centre of attention.
  • Colours: Sliver, Apricot, Fawn and Black.
  • Average Life Span: 12 to 15 years.
  • Best Age to Buy: Never ever accept a puppy less than 7 weeks of age, it is vitally important they are with their mother and siblings up to this age. Puppies over twelve weeks should be avoided if possible.
  • Possible Medical Issues:eye issues, hip dysplasia, Pug Dog Encephalitis.
  • Pros: Pugs love to be around their owner and will happily sit on your lap and sleep in your bed with you. The Pug is a dog that craves attention, and is well socialized, can be curious about other people.
  • Cons:
  • Advise: It’s best to choose the middle puppy, the one that’s not fighting its siblings or hiding in the corner.
